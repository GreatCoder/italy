Ciao - Welcome to Italy!

About

Italy is one of the most popular travel destinations in the world. It has everything: Magnificent views, historic sites, famous museums, excellent food, top fashion designs, and of course - beautiful women. No matter how long your vacation is, you will still have to choose between several options when planning each day. There are just so many amazing places to visit, that you can't get to all of them, or even most.

Population

Over 60 million people live in Italy. The most populated cities are:

  1. Rome
  2. Milan
  3. Naples
  4. Turin
  5. Palermo
  6. Genoa

Regions

Italy is subdivided into 20 regions. Here are some of the most known regions:

  • Lombardy
  • Lazio
  • Campania
  • Veneto
  • Emilia-Romagna
  • Piedmont
  • Apulia
